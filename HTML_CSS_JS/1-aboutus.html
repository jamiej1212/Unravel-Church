EACM

Our Network

EACM stands for Evangel Association of
Churches & Ministries. It is a worldwide network of
independent yet connected churches, ministries and ministers.

Unravel Church

Our church is a non-denominational church.
The doctrine is modeled after the ministry of Jesus,
as in the words of the four Gospels.

· Declaration of the Word
· Soul salvation
· Raising disciples

Acts 1:8 (NIV)

But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you,
and you will be my witnesses in Jerusalem, and in all Judea
and Samaria, and to the ends of the earth.

· Let us become witnesses of Jesus and evangelize to the
ends of the earth. This is the motif of Unravel Church.
